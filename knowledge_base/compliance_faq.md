# Compliance & Security FAQ

## Purpose

This document provides approved guidance regarding privacy, security, regulatory compliance, data processing practices, and enterprise procurement requirements.

Support representatives and AI agents should use this document when responding to compliance-related inquiries.

This document is informational and does not replace legal review when required.

---

# 1. GDPR Frequently Asked Questions

## What is GDPR?

The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) is a privacy regulation governing the processing of personal data for individuals located within the European Economic Area (EEA).

The company supports GDPR-related customer requests through its compliance operations process.

---

## How can a customer request access to their data?

Customers may submit a data access request through support channels.

Required actions:

* Verify customer identity.
* Create a compliance ticket.
* Record request date.
* Route request to Compliance Operations.

---

## How can a customer request deletion of personal data?

Customers may request deletion of personal information where legally permissible.

Required actions:

* Verify identity.
* Create compliance review ticket.
* Review retention obligations.
* Process approved deletion request.

Human review is always required.

---

## What is a GDPR Article 20 Request?

Article 20 concerns the right to data portability.

Customers may request a machine-readable copy of their personal data.

Required actions:

* Create compliance ticket.
* Verify identity.
* Route request to Compliance Operations.
* Acknowledge receipt and cite the 30 calendar day response window.

Target response time:

Within 30 calendar days.

AI agents may draft an acknowledgement for human review, but must not treat formal GDPR requests as routine customer inquiries.

# 2. Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

## Is a DPA available?

Yes.

A standard Data Processing Agreement (DPA) is available for business and enterprise customers.

---

## How is a DPA requested?

Required actions:

* Create compliance request.
* Verify customer organization.
* Route request to Compliance Operations.

Support representatives may provide the standard DPA request process but may not negotiate contract terms.

---

# 3. HIPAA Compliance

## Does the platform support HIPAA-regulated customers?

Yes.

Enterprise customers may use the platform in HIPAA-sensitive environments when an approved Business Associate Agreement (BAA) is executed.

---

## Is a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) available?

Yes.

BAAs are available only for Enterprise-tier customers.

All BAA requests must be reviewed by Compliance Operations.

---

## Can support teams approve HIPAA-related commitments?

No.

Only authorized compliance personnel may approve HIPAA-related contractual commitments.

Support representatives and AI agents must escalate such requests.

---

# 4. SOC 2 Compliance

## Does the company maintain SOC 2 certification?

Yes.

The company maintains SOC 2 Type II compliance.

---

## Can customers receive SOC 2 reports?

Enterprise customers may request access to approved compliance documentation.

Required actions:

* Verify customer organization.
* Create compliance request.
* Route request for approval.

Confidential reports should never be distributed automatically.

Examples:

# 8. Escalation Requirements

Immediate escalation is required for:

* GDPR requests
* HIPAA contractual requests
* DPA negotiations
* Regulatory inquiries
* Audit requests
* Government requests

Required actions:

1. Create compliance ticket.
2. Record request details.
3. Preserve communication history.
4. Route to Compliance Operations.

---

# 9. Agent Guidance

AI agents handling compliance-related requests should:

1. Identify the applicable compliance category.
2. Verify whether human review is required.
3. Create a compliance ticket when necessary.
4. Preserve thread history.
5. Escalate contractual or regulatory matters.
6. Avoid making legal commitments.

Agents may provide informational guidance based on approved policy but must not approve legal, regulatory, or contractual requests.

When uncertainty exists, route the case for human review.
